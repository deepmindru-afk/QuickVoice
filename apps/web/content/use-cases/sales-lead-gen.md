## The Math Behind AI-Powered Sales Outreach

A human SDR makes 40–60 cold calls per day. With voicemail, gatekeepers, and connection rates of 5–8%, that yields 2–4 live conversations per day. At $60,000/year all-in, you're paying $82–$123 per live conversation.

An AI voice agent makes 200–1,000 calls per day (depending on configuration), with the same or better connection rates, at a cost of $0.25–$1.00 per connected call. At 200 calls/day with a 7% connection rate, you get 14 live conversations per day — 3.5–7x more than a human SDR at one-fifth the cost.

This isn't about replacing salespeople. It's about letting salespeople do what they do best — build relationships, handle objections, close deals — while AI handles the top-of-funnel volume work.

## Sales and Lead Gen Use Cases for AI Voice Agents

### Outbound Prospecting

AI agents work through prospect lists at scale:
- Call through high-volume prospect lists (trade show leads, purchased lists, website visitors)
- Deliver a concise, personalized opening message
- Qualify interest with 2–3 questions
- Schedule a discovery call with a human sales rep for interested prospects
- Handle objections with pre-approved responses ("I understand — when would be a better time?")
- Log all outcomes to CRM automatically

Best use cases: home services, insurance, real estate, financial products, B2B SaaS with SMB target market, franchises, staffing.

### Inbound Lead Response

When inbound leads come in via web form, call, or chat, response speed is everything. AI agents call back within 90 seconds:
- "Hi, this is a call from [Company] — I noticed you just submitted a request on our website for [service/product]. I wanted to reach out while I had you in mind. Is now a good time for 2 minutes?"
- Pre-qualify and schedule a meeting with the appropriate sales rep
- Eliminate the "lead goes cold while rep is busy" problem entirely

### Lead Qualification for Complex Pipelines

AI agents conduct structured qualification calls to assess BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline):
- "What's driving your interest in [product/service] right now?"
- "What does your timeline look like for making a decision?"
- "Who else would be involved in evaluating this?"
- "What's your budget range for a solution like this?"

Responses are captured, scored, and leads are triaged: hot (immediate follow-up by senior rep), warm (add to nurture sequence), cold (remove from active pipeline).

### Multi-Touch Follow-Up Sequences

Most sales require 5–8 touchpoints before a prospect agrees to a meeting. AI agents handle the follow-up sequence:
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up if no response
- Day 7: Second follow-up with a new value angle
- Day 14: Final check-in before disqualifying

This persistent follow-up — which human SDRs rarely execute consistently due to volume — increases connection rate by 35–55%.

### Event and Webinar Follow-Up

After a trade show, webinar, or marketing event, you have a warm lead list that goes cold within 72 hours. AI agents call every contact within 24 hours:
- Reference the specific event: "We met at the Chicago home show last weekend"
- Gauge interest level
- Schedule follow-up meeting while interest is highest

### Win-Back Campaigns

Former customers who churned or competitors you lost to are some of the highest-conversion prospects in any sales pipeline. AI agents run win-back campaigns:
- Acknowledge the previous relationship
- Introduce a new value proposition or product improvement
- Offer an exclusive win-back incentive
- Schedule a reintroduction call with an account executive

## AI vs. Human SDR: The Hybrid Approach

The highest-performing sales organizations in 2026 use AI for the top of the funnel and humans for everything after:

1. **AI handles:** Prospecting, initial qualification, meeting scheduling
2. **Human handles:** Discovery calls, demos, proposals, negotiations, closing

This hybrid approach lets a 10-person sales team behave like a 30-person team in terms of pipeline generation, while maintaining the human touch for the high-value parts of the sale.

SDRs in this model focus 80% of their time on hot inbound calls and pre-qualified meetings — instead of cold calling. Conversion from meeting to opportunity increases by 35–50% because every meeting is pre-qualified.
