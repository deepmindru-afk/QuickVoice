# Code Of Conduct

QuickVoice should be a useful, direct, and respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat contributors with respect.
- Give technical feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Keep competitive comparisons factual, relevant, and respectful.
- Welcome contributors regardless of background, identity, location, or experience level.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Demeaning language or repeated hostile comments.
- Derailing project spaces with competitor bashing or unverifiable claims.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors whose behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the project owner on GitHub or through the contact channels listed on [quickvoice.co](https://quickvoice.co).
